>

> Fever · severe muscle aches · headache · fatigue · GI symptoms

### POST 6 — Plain-English exposure explainer

> How does someone get Andes virus?
>
> 1. Inhaling air contaminated with rodent droppings, urine, or saliva
> 2. Direct contact with rodents or their nests
> 3. Touching contaminated surfaces, then your face

> Cleaning rodent areas? Don't sweep. Don't vacuum. Wear a mask.
